How to play Sudoku
• Fill the grid so that each row, column, and 3×3 box contains the digits 1–9 exactly once.
• Black numbers are the given clues (locked).
• Blue numbers are the ones you place by drag & drop.
• Drag digits from the number bank into empty cells. You can also drag blue numbers between cells.
• Double-click a blue number to clear it. Double-click an empty cell to clear all its notes.
• Turn on Notes to place/remove pencil marks by dragging digits into cells.
